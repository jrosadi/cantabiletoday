About Us

Cantabile Today is a freelance musician service founded out of a passion for music. We enjoy bringing beautiful music to your special events. We also love to bring the joy of music to our community with our pro bono services, such as playing at retirement homes. We can arrange music for solo violin and two violins. Please contact us to see how we can help you with your special events.
